pub struct SecretCollection(_);

SecretCollection represents a collection of secret items stored in the Secret Service. A collection can be in a locked or unlocked state. Use Lock::lock() or Lock::unlock() to lock or unlock the collection. Use get_items() to lookup the items in the collection. There may not be any items exposed when the collection is locked.
